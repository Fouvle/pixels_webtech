How Scoring Works

Products are evaluated across five key categories, with each category contributing to the final sustainability score.

Ingredient Sourcing & Safety

30% of total score

  • Biodegradability: Ingredients must break down naturally without harming ecosystems (10 points)
  • Sustainable Sourcing: Raw materials must be renewably and ethically sourced (8 points)
  • Non-toxic: Ingredients must be safe for humans and aquatic life (7 points)
  • Organic Certification: Preference for certified organic ingredients (5 points)
